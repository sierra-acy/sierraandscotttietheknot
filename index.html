Our Love Story

Groom

Scavenging for Love

We met the week before classes started at UT in a Computer Science Boot Camp for incoming freshmen. At the end of the week, there was a campus-wide scavenger hunt. Scott's original team disbanded, leaving him all alone. He approached Sierra's team and asked to join, and we were attached from that moment on.

Romance in Walmart

Though not "official," we consider our many trips to Walmart to be our first dates. We'd take the bus to Walmart, have lunch in the shopping center, buy some hotpockets and ramen, and head home for a nap. You couldn't ask for a more romantic start.

The Proposal

On September 22, 2019, our 6th anniversary of dating, Scott planned a small hike to the lighthouse at Discovery Park. It was the typical Seattle day: cloudy, raining, and chilly. When we got to the lighthouse, we sat on a log and talked about how great we are. Scott had this whole speech prepared, but Sierra thought we were just having a conversation. Sierra still had no idea! We have adorable engagement photos of us all rained on, wearing ponchos, and red-nosed from the cold. A very PNW engagement indeed!

Love in the Time of COVID

Being locked up together during COVID was surprisingly great! We were both able to work from home, and for the first time we were able to have lunch together, eat dinner before 8pm, and have several extra hours to hang out together after work without the commute. We weren't worried about it before, but this lockdown confirmed that we never get tired of hanging out together!

The First Wedding Day

After a year and a half engagement and rescheduling the wedding, we decided it was time to be married! On June 11, 2021, we had a small courthouse wedding with our witnesses and families in attendance. Though we were bummed to have to move our big fancy wedding, this is the kind of wedding Scott wanted in the first place, so now it's a win-win!

The Fancy Wedding Day

It's finally time! We are so excited to finally celebrate with our friends and families on July 29, 2022!
